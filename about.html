About Us

Creating Extraordinary Travel Experiences Since 2010

Welcome to LuxTravel DMC

Founded in 2010, LuxTravel DMC has established itself as a premier destination management company specializing in luxury travel experiences across the globe. Our founder, Elizabeth Chen, started the company with a simple vision: to create travel experiences that transform lives while providing unparalleled luxury and attention to detail.

Today, we are proud to serve discerning travelers from around the world, from celebrities and executives to families seeking extraordinary vacations. Our headquarters in New York is supported by regional offices in London, Dubai, and Singapore, allowing us to provide on-the-ground expertise in key luxury destinations.

Our Philosophy

At LuxTravel DMC, we believe that true luxury is defined by personalization, exclusivity, and authenticity. We don't simply book hotels and flights—we craft journeys that reflect your unique interests, preferences, and dreams.

We understand that your time is precious, and vacations are opportunities for rejuvenation, connection, and discovery. That's why each itinerary we create is treated as a work of art—carefully crafted, thoughtfully designed, and flawlessly executed.

Most importantly, we believe that luxury travel should create lasting memories and meaningful connections with the places you visit and the people you meet along the way.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and booking process

For luxury tours, especially during peak travel seasons, we recommend booking 6-12 months in advance to ensure availability of top accommodations and exclusive experiences. However, our team can also accommodate last-minute bookings with our extensive network of partners.

Our luxury packages typically include premium accommodations, private transportation, expert guides, exclusive experiences, daily breakfast, select meals at recommended restaurants, and 24/7 concierge support. Each itinerary is customized to your preferences and can include additional services as requested.

After contacting us through this form, one of our travel experts will schedule a consultation to understand your preferences, interests, and travel style. Based on this conversation, we'll create a tailored itinerary that you can further refine until it perfectly matches your vision.

We accept major credit cards, bank transfers, and select digital payment methods. For most bookings, we require a 25% deposit to secure your reservation, with the balance due 60 days prior to departure. Flexible payment plans are available for early bookings.
